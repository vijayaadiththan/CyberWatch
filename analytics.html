CyberWatch Intelligence Insights

Key Observations

The professionalization of ransomware continues to be the dominant trend, with Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS) lowering the barrier to entry for lower-tier threat actors while sophisticated groups focus on high-value "big game" targets.

Emerging Trends

Generative AI is increasingly utilized by threat actors to automate highly personalized phishing campaigns at scale and to accelerate the discovery of software vulnerabilities.

Lessons from History

Past incidents like SolarWinds and Log4Shell underscore the critical fragility of the global digital supply chain. Modern defense must prioritize "blind spot" visibility in third-party software dependencies.

Future Threat Outlook

"We anticipate a shift toward edge-computing exploitation and the weaponization of automated response protocols, where attackers trick AI defenders into shutting down legitimate business processes."
